Key Insights

From the research, three core needs emerged that became the foundation for the app's features.

Students need an application that provides easy access to various learning materials.

Task and deadline reminders are crucial for helping students manage their time effectively.

Students want to track their progress and achievements to stay motivated.

Design Process (Ideation)

A brainstorming process was conducted to generate feature ideas for Learnify. These ideas were then validated through discussions with potential users. Some of the initial concepts included:

  • Learning: Providing study materials, video lessons, and practice exercises.
  • Tasks: Featuring deadline reminders, a monthly preview, and a feature to create new to-do lists.
  • Calendar: A calendar with event reminders and the ability to add new events.
  • Progress Tracker: Recording task progress, whether completed on time, late, in progress, or not finished.
